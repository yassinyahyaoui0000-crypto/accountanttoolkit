Editorial policy

This site tries to prioritize practical software fit, current official-source checks, and honest pricing caution over affiliate-style certainty theater.

Updated March 19, 2026

Short version

Official sources first, softer copy when pricing is unstable, and no fake freshness.

How pricing claims are handled

When a vendor uses introductory discounts, seat-based pricing, region-based pricing, or usage-based billing, the page should say that clearly instead of pretending a single static number tells the whole story.

How updates work

Pages should show a meaningful updated or reviewed date when a real content review has happened. Date changes should reflect actual review work, not artificial freshness.

How corrections work

Factual corrections should be made promptly, especially when they affect pricing, trials, plan names, or a recommendation outcome.
