# 90-Day Non-Social Traffic Playbook

This playbook is the working version of the non-social growth plan for `AccountantToolkit`.

## Goal

Grow US organic traffic without relying on LinkedIn, X, Reddit, Quora, or other social posting.

The operating priority is:

1. turn existing impressions into clicks
2. publish only inside clusters already moving
3. use `statement-to-csv` as the easiest outreach and link asset

## Days 1-14

- Publish no broad new article batches.
- Review Search Console with the `United States` filter first.
- Ignore Tunisia testing traffic in business judgment.
- Refresh only the pages already earning impressions or sitting in positions `7-25`.
- Recheck canonical consistency and `.html` cleanup on the known legacy pages.

## Days 15-45

- Publish at most `1` new page every `7-10` days.
- Stay inside the winning order:
  - receipt capture support pages first
  - bookkeeper / solo-accountant support pages second
  - invoicing pages only if that cluster starts moving materially
- Every new page must strengthen one existing winner and link back into a hub, a review, and a comparison.
- Keep `statement-to-csv` visible as a separate tool lane.

## Days 46-90

- Double down on the cluster with the clearest US impressions and rankings.
- Use the tool as the main outreach asset before pitching affiliate pages.
- Add `2-3` supporting tool-intent pages from `docs/statement2csv-support-queue.md`.
- Keep rewriting titles and snippet openings on moving pages before adding breadth.

## Weekly review cadence

Every 7 days, review:

- US impressions by page
- US clicks by page
- pages in positions `8-20`
- top US queries by impressions
- indexed count vs `sitemap.xml`
- CTA clicks on top commercial pages
- whether `statement-to-csv` is earning impressions, links, or mention-worthy utility traffic

## Targets

- By `2026-04-30`: first real US clicks and at least `2-3` pages sitting in positions `8-20`
- By `2026-05-31`: one clear winner cluster and repeat US clicks
- By `2026-06-30`: several pages near page 1 and the tool earning support-page visibility, mentions, or links

## Warnings

- If there are still no real US clicks by `2026-04-30`, stop new publishing and do a pure CTR + ranking sprint.
- If only the receipt cluster moves, stop spreading effort evenly across weaker lanes.
- If outreach fails for 6 weeks, narrow the targets and pitch the tool, not the affiliate content.
