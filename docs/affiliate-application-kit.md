## Suggested publisher description

`AccountantToolkit is a niche editorial site covering accounting, bookkeeping, invoicing, receipt-capture, and workflow software for freelancers, solo accountants, and bookkeepers. We publish buyer-intent roundups, comparisons, and reviews that send readers from shortlist to final vendor decision.`

## Suggested traffic and promotion description

`Our growth plan is SEO-led. We publish product comparisons, review pages, and buyer guides, then measure vendor click-through via GA4 and GTM. Approved programs are added only to relevant pages after editorial review.`

## Suggested compliance description

`We maintain visible trust pages, a public editorial policy, and a public affiliate disclosure. Product claims are checked against official vendor pages and we do not promise fixed pricing when vendors use promotional or region-specific offers.`

## If a program asks how links are managed

Answer with:

`Commercial links are centralized in the site script so we can switch approved tracking URLs on per product without rewriting editorial copy across the site. Unapproved products stay on official vendor links until approval is granted.`

- Keep tracking the clicks anyway.
- Reapply after you have Search Console impressions, outbound click data, and a clearer niche focus.
