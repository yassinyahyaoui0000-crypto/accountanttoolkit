This page explains what kinds of messages AccountantToolkit is set up to handle. The repository does not include a confirmed public inbox yet, so the preferred public contact email or form still needs to be supplied by the site owner before launch.

Updated March 19, 2026 Trust page

Best use of this page

Use it to define corrections, partnership, and technical-contact scope now, then publish the final public contact method before launch.

What kinds of messages belong here

  • Factual corrections to software, pricing, trial, or integration claims.
  • Broken link reports or technical issues on the site.
  • Questions about affiliate disclosure or editorial standards.
  • Business inquiries that are relevant to the site’s software coverage.

What still needs to be added before launch

A public contact method is not present in this repository. Publish the preferred email address or contact form here before launch so readers have a clear path for corrections and business inquiries.

If you do add a public inbox later, keep the message scope narrow: corrections, technical issues, disclosure questions, and relevant business inquiries.
