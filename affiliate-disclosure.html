Affiliate disclosure

Product links on the site currently point to official vendor homepages. If affiliate links are approved later, they should be disclosed clearly and should not change rankings or verdicts.

Updated March 19, 2026

Core rule

Commercial relationships should not be the reason a tool is recommended.

How commercial links should work

If the site earns a commission from a product link in the future, that relationship should be disclosed clearly near the content and supported by this site-wide page.

What should not change

  • Recommendations should not be rewritten to fit commission opportunities.
  • Weaknesses and pricing caveats should not be hidden.
  • Readers should still be sent to the official vendor site for current pricing checks.
